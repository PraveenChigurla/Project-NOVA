# Project NOVA

## Engineering Commencement

**Research Phase:** COMPLETE

**Engineering Validation Program:** ACTIVE

Today marks the transition of Project NOVA from architectural research to engineering execution.

The architecture has been specified.

The standards have been frozen.

The constitutional principles have been established.

Future progress will no longer be measured by the number of new frameworks introduced.

Instead, it will be measured by evidence.

Success will be demonstrated through:

* Reliable benchmarks.
* Security validation.
* Production-quality implementations.
* Extension ecosystem growth.
* Real-world usability.
* Long-term maintainability.

Every implementation shall be evaluated against the NOVA Standard.

Every benchmark shall strengthen confidence in the architecture.

Every defect shall become an opportunity to improve the implementation—not to abandon the principles.

The mission of the Engineering Validation Program is simple:

**Prove that the NOVA Standard works in reality.**

When evidence contradicts implementation, improve the implementation.

When evidence contradicts assumptions, investigate the assumptions.

When evidence confirms the architecture, preserve it.

Project NOVA now enters its engineering era.

Research established the principles.

Engineering will prove them.
